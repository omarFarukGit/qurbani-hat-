# 🐄 Qurbani Hat – Online Livestock Marketplace

Qurbani Hat একটি অনলাইন প্ল্যাটফর্ম যেখানে ব্যবহারকারীরা সহজে কোরবানির পশু ব্রাউজ, ফিল্টার এবং ক্রয় করতে পারে। এই প্রজেক্টে ইউজাররা price, category এবং অন্যান্য তথ্য অনুযায়ী পশু খুঁজতে পারে।

Online Qurbani Hat is a modern digital platform where users can select, book, and complete their Qurbani (sacrifice) from home.

On this platform, different types of animals (such as cows, goats, and sheep) are displayed with images, videos, weight, price, and detailed information, allowing buyers to easily compare and choose the right one. Users can make payments online to book an animal, and the Qurbani is performed according to Islamic guidelines on the scheduled day. After that, the meat is processed and delivered to the customer’s address.

The main goal of this platform is to make the entire Qurbani process simple, time-saving, safe, and hassle-free—especially for busy city residents and people living abroad.

👉 In short:
Online Qurbani Hat = A complete digital solution for performing Qurbani easily from home
